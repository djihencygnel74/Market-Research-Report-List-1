<p><p>The server backup market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based backups involve storing data off-site on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and automated updates, ideal for businesses seeking flexibility. Conversely, on-premises backups keep data within the organization's physical infrastructure, providing users with direct control and security, but often requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Each solution has distinct advantages, catering to different organizational needs and risk management strategies.</p></p>
