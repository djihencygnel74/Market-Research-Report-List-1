<p><p>The Lidar window market comprises two main types: mechanical scanning Lidar and solid-state Lidar. Mechanical scanning Lidar utilizes rotating components to capture 3D images, providing high-resolution data over wide areas, often used in topographic mapping and autonomous vehicles. In contrast, solid-state Lidar employs fixed optics and electronic scanning, making it more compact and durable, suitable for applications requiring robust performance in various environmental conditions. Both technologies are integral in enhancing precision for industries like automotive, agriculture, and surveying.</p></p>
