<p><p>The obstructive sleep apnea therapeutic devices market includes several key types. Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) devices, such as CPAP and BiPAP, deliver continuous air pressure to keep airways open during sleep. Facial interfaces, including masks and nasal pillows, are essential components that ensure proper air delivery. Adaptive Servo-Ventilation (ASV) adjusts pressure based on detected airflow patterns, providing a personalized treatment approach. Other devices in the market may include oral appliances and positional therapy aids, targeting various patient needs for effective sleep apnea management.</p></p>
