<p><strong>The Connected Car Mobility Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V2I</li><li>V2V</li><li>V2C</li><li>V2P</li><li>V2X</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected car mobility solutions encompass various communication types enhancing vehicle interactions. V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure) enables cars to communicate with traffic signals and road signs for improved navigation. V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle) facilitates direct communication between vehicles to prevent collisions and optimize traffic flow. V2C (Vehicle-to-Cloud) connects vehicles to cloud services for data sharing and processing. V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian) enhances safety by alerting drivers about nearby pedestrians. V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) integrates all these interactions, enabling more seamless and efficient transport ecosystems.</p></p>
