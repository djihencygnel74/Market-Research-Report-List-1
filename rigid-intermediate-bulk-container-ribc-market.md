<p><p>Rigid Intermediate Bulk Containers (RIBCs) are industrial-grade containers used for transporting and storing bulk materials. The market for RIBCs is divided into two main types: lined and unlined. Lined RIBCs have an internal lining that protects the contents from contamination and provides added barrier properties, making them suitable for chemical or sensitive materials. Unlined RIBCs, on the other hand, are used for non-reactive substances and offer simpler, cost-effective solutions but may expose contents to environmental factors.</p></p>
