<p><p>The vehicle hydraulic brake fluid market comprises several types, primarily DOT3, DOT4, and DOT5, along with other specialty fluids. DOT3 is a glycol-based fluid suitable for most vehicles, offering a good balance of performance and cost. DOT4 provides higher boiling points, making it ideal for performance vehicles. DOT5, a silicone-based fluid, offers excellent temperature stability and is non-hygroscopic but is not compatible with glycol fluids. Other types include specialized formulations for specific applications, enhancing performance and safety in braking systems.</p></p>
