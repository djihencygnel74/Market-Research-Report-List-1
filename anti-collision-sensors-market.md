<p><p>Anti-collision sensors are crucial for enhancing safety in various applications by preventing collisions. The market for these sensors includes infrared and laser sensors. Infrared sensors detect obstacles by emitting infrared light and measuring reflections, making them effective for short-range applications. In contrast, laser sensors utilize laser beams for precise distance measurement and obstacle detection, offering longer-range capabilities and higher accuracy. Both types are widely used in automotive, robotics, and industrial sectors to improve safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
