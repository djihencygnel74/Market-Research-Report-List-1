<p><ul><li>Hard Tag</li><li>Soft Tag</li><li>Deactivator or Detacher</li><li>Detection System</li><li>Permanent Deactivation Tags</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) systems include several key market types: </p><p>1. **Hard Tags** are durable, visible security devices attached to high-value items, designed to deter theft. </p><p>2. **Soft Tags**, often made of labels or stickers, are discreet and suitable for lower-value merchandise. </p><p>3. **Deactivators or Detachers** remove or deactivate security tags at checkout. </p><p>4. **Detection Systems** consist of antennas or sensors that identify tagged items at exits. </p><p>5. **Permanent Deactivation Tags** are used for items that will not be sold again, ensuring they remain non-functional after purchase.</p></p>
