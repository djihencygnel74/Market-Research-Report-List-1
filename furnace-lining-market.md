<p><p>The furnace lining market is categorized into three main types based on chemical resistance and application: acidic, basic, and neutral types. Acidic linings are designed to withstand high temperatures and corrosion from acidic materials, often used in industries like steel and glass manufacturing. Basic linings resist alkaline substances, ideal for non-ferrous metal industries. Neutral linings provide a balanced solution, suitable for various applications where both acidic and basic materials are processed, ensuring versatility in furnace operations.</p></p>
