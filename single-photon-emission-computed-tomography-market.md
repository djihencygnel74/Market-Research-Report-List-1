<p><p>Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) equipment is categorized into two main types: fixed type and rotary type. Fixed type SPECT systems are stationary and designed for specific imaging applications, offering high stability and ease of use. In contrast, rotary type systems feature a rotating gantry that enhances imaging flexibility, allowing for a wider range of diagnostic capabilities. Both types serve critical roles in nuclear medicine, providing detailed insights into organ function and aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions.</p></p>
