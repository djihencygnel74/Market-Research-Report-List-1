<p><p>Silicon Carbide (SiC) MOSFETs are categorized primarily by their channel configuration. N-Channel MOSFETs are used for high-efficiency applications, known for better conduction and switching performance. Dual N-Channel variants incorporate two N-Channel devices, enhancing power density and reducing footprint. Quad N-Channel MOSFETs combine four channels to optimize performance in compact designs, suitable for advanced applications. Six N-Channel configurations further amplify power handling and efficiency, making them ideal for demanding environments in industries like automotive, telecommunications, and renewable energy.</p></p>
