<p><p>X-ray line detectors are critical in various imaging applications, and they are categorized into three main types. Single-energy detectors capture images at a single energy level, providing basic material differentiation. Dual-energy detectors utilize two distinct energy levels to enhance contrast and material identification, making them suitable for complex imaging tasks. Multi-energy detectors leverage multiple energy levels, offering superior analytical capabilities for detailed composition analysis. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of X-ray imaging processes.</p></p>
