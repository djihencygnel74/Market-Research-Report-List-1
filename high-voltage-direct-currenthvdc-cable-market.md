The ability of HVDC systems to efficiently transmit electricity over long distances makes them increasingly attractive in modern energy infrastructures focused on sustainability.</p><p>The HVDC cable market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the global push for renewable energy integration and the need for enhanced grid stability.
