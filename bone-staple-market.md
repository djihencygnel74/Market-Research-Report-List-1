<p><ul><li>Non-Absorbable Bone Staple</li><li>Absorbable Bone Staple</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone staple market is categorized into two main types: non-absorbable and absorbable bone staples. Non-absorbable bone staples are made from permanent materials like titanium or stainless steel, providing long-term stabilization for fractures or orthopedic surgeries. In contrast, absorbable bone staples are designed to gradually dissolve and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal surgery. Both types are essential in orthopedic procedures, enhancing surgical outcomes while addressing varying patient needs and recovery processes.</p></p>
