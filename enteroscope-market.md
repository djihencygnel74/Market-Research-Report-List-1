<p><p>The enteroscope market encompasses two primary types: video endoscopes and fiberscopes. Video endoscopes utilize advanced imaging technology, providing high-definition visuals for enhanced diagnosis and therapeutic procedures within the gastrointestinal tract. Their digital capabilities allow for easier documentation and real-time analysis. In contrast, fiberscopes employ a flexible fiber optic design, enabling access to hard-to-reach areas with sufficient visualization. Both types are crucial for minimally invasive procedures, diagnostics, and treatment of various gastrointestinal disorders, catering to the evolving needs of healthcare professionals.</p></p>
