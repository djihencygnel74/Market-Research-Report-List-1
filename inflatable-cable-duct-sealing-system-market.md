<p><p>The Inflatable Cable Duct Sealing System market includes two key types: "Empty Pipeline" and "Multicore Cable." The Empty Pipeline type is designed to seal ducts that are currently vacant, preventing the ingress of water or contaminants during installation or maintenance. In contrast, the Multicore Cable system is specifically engineered for ducts containing multiple cables, ensuring a tight seal around each cable to protect against environmental factors. Both types enhance infrastructure reliability and reduce maintenance costs in cable management systems.</p></p>
