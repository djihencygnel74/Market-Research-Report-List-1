<p><p>The dental engraving and milling machine market is categorized primarily into two types: Four Axis and Five Axis machines. Four Axis machines operate with three linear axes and one rotational axis, making them suitable for straightforward designs and moderate complexity in dental restorations. In contrast, Five Axis machines provide additional flexibility by incorporating a second rotational axis, allowing for intricate shapes and more precise restorations. This capability enhances efficiency and accuracy, catering to advanced dental applications and diverse material uses.</p></p>
