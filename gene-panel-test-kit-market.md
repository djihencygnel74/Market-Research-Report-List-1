<p><p>The Gene Panel Test Kit market encompasses various approaches to genetic testing, primarily focused on evaluating multiple genes simultaneously for disease predisposition or therapeutic responses. The amplicon-based approach involves generating specific DNA fragments through PCR amplification, enabling targeted sequencing of selected genes. Conversely, the hybridization-based approach relies on complementary binding of DNA probes to target sequences, facilitating the identification of genetic variants. Both methods enhance precision in diagnostics and personalized medicine, catering to the growing demand for comprehensive genetic insights.</p></p>
