<p><p>The power dividers market is categorized into passive and active power dividers. Passive power dividers distribute input power evenly without amplification, ensuring minimal signal loss, commonly used in RF applications and telecommunications. Active power dividers, on the other hand, utilize amplification to boost the signal strength, making them suitable for applications requiring higher power levels and improved performance. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including broadcasting, aerospace, and wireless communication, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
