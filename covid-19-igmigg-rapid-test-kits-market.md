<p><strong>The COVID 19 IgMIgG Rapid Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RDT(Rapid Diagnostic Test)</li><li>ELISA(Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Aassay)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG rapid test kits market includes various types, each serving distinct purposes. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results using a simple procedure, ideal for on-the-spot testing. Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) offer higher sensitivity and specificity, suitable for laboratory settings, allowing for detailed quantitative analysis of antibodies. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral activity, providing critical insights into immune response, particularly for vaccine efficacy and infection studies.</p></p>
