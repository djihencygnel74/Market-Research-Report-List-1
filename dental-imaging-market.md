<p><strong>The Dental Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extraoral Imaging</li><li>Intraoral Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging market is primarily divided into two types: extraoral and intraoral imaging. Extraoral imaging involves capturing images of the entire jaw and skull, typically using panoramic radiographs or cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT), providing a comprehensive view of dental and skeletal structures. In contrast, intraoral imaging focuses on individual teeth and surrounding tissues, employing techniques like bitewing, periapical, and intraoral cameras. Both imaging types are crucial for diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring oral health.</p></p>
