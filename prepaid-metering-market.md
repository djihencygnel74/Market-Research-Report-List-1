<p><p>Prepaid metering refers to a system where users pay for their utility consumption in advance, ensuring they have access to services such as electricity, gas, or water until their prepaid balance is exhausted. The trend towards smart metering technologies is also noteworthy, as these devices enable remote monitoring and management, enhancing user convenience and operational efficiency. </p><p>Furthermore, government initiatives promoting energy efficiency and sustainable practices are increasingly supporting the adoption of prepaid metering systems. The growing integration of IoT and advanced analytics into metering solutions is reinforcing these trends, leading to enhanced decision-making and customer engagement.
