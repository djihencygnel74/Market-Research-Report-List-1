<p><ul><li>Immunochromatography</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pepsinogen Test kit market includes two primary types: immunochromatography and chemiluminescence immunoassay. Immunochromatography is a rapid diagnostic method that uses a simple strip format for qualitative results, often providing immediate feedback. In contrast, chemiluminescence immunoassay utilizes chemical reactions to produce light, allowing for higher sensitivity and quantification of pepsinogen levels. Both methods are essential for assessing gastric health and identifying conditions such as atrophic gastritis or gastric cancer, catering to various patient needs and laboratory settings.</p></p>
