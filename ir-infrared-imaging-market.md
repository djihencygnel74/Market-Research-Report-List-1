<p><strong>The IR (Infrared) Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared (Swir) Imaging</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (Mwir) Imaging</li><li>Long Wave Infrared (Lwir) Imaging</li><li>Wave Infrared (Fwir) Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared imaging market is categorized into four main types based on wavelength: </p><p>1. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) imaging, which ranges from 1 to 3 micrometers and is used in applications like agriculture and surveillance due to its sensitivity to moisture.</p><p>2. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) imaging, spanning 3 to 5 micrometers, is commonly used for thermal imaging and industrial inspections.</p><p>3. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) imaging, from 8 to 14 micrometers, excels in detecting heat emissions and is prevalent in building diagnostics and military applications.</p><p>4. Far Infrared (FIR) imaging, beyond 14 micrometers, is less common but finds use in scientific research and certain medical applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on their sensitivity and wavelength characteristics.</p></p>
