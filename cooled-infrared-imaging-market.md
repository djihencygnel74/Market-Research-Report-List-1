<p><strong>The Cooled Infrared Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR</li><li>Mid Wave IR</li><li>Long Wave IR</li><li>Far Wave IR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooled infrared imaging market is segmented into four types based on wavelength ranges. </p><p>Short Wave IR (SWIR) captures wavelengths from 0.9 to 1.7 microns, ideal for detecting moisture and identifying materials. Mid Wave IR (MWIR) operates between 3 to 5 microns, commonly used for thermal imaging in defense and industrial applications. Long Wave IR (LWIR) spans 8 to 12 microns, effective for sensing temperature variations. Far Wave IR focuses on wavelengths above 12 microns, primarily utilized in specialized scientific and industrial monitoring applications.</p></p>
