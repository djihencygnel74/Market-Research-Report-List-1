<p><p>The intraoral dental scanner market is categorized into two main types: wired scanners and wireless scanners. Wired scanners connect directly to a computer or workstation, offering consistent power and data transfer but limiting mobility. In contrast, wireless scanners utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology, providing greater flexibility for practitioners and enhancing patient comfort by reducing cable clutter. Both types serve the essential function of capturing detailed digital impressions, aiding in diagnostics, treatment planning, and the creation of dental restorations.</p></p>
