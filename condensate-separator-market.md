<p><p>The condensate separator market can be categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic systems. In contrast, fully automatic condensate separators operate independently, using sensors and automated controls to optimize performance and maintenance, making them ideal for larger facilities with high condensate volumes. Both types aim to efficiently separate condensate from oil and gas streams, improving operational efficiency and environmental compliance.</p></p>
