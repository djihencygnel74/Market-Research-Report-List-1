<p><p>The eye examination equipment market comprises various types, including phoropters, autorefractors, lensmeters, and others. Phoropters are used to determine refractive errors by presenting a series of lens options to patients. Autorefractors automatically assess vision by measuring how light alters as it passes through the eye. Lensmeters measure the prescription of eyeglass lenses accurately. Other equipment includes tools like slit lamps and retinoscopes, which aid in comprehensive eye assessments, contributing to advancements in ophthalmic care and vision correction.</p></p>
