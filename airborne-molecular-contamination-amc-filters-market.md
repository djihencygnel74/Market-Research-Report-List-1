<p><strong>The Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemisorption Filters (Activated Carbon or Aluminas)</li><li>Bonded Media Panels (Activated Carbon Formed into Monolithic (Single-Piece) Panels)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airborne Molecular Contamination (AMC) filters market consists of various types designed to mitigate airborne pollutants in sensitive environments. Chemisorption filters utilize activated carbon or aluminas to chemically react with and capture harmful molecules. Bonded media panels, on the other hand, are created by forming activated carbon into monolithic panels, offering a rigid structure that enhances filtration efficiency and minimizes airflow resistance. Both types are crucial for maintaining cleanroom standards in industries like semiconductor manufacturing and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
