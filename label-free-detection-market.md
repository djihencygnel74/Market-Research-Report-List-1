<p><p>Label-free detection technologies, such as Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) and Bio-layer Interferometry (BLI), are crucial in the biosensing market for their ability to monitor biomolecular interactions without the use of labels or dyes. SPR detects changes in the refractive index near a sensor surface in response to binding events, while BLI measures shifts in interference patterns caused by biomolecule binding on a biosensor surface. Both methods provide real-time analysis, making them vital for research, diagnostics, and drug development applications.</p></p>
