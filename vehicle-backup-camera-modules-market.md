<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle backup camera module market is primarily categorized into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and low noise, making them ideal for low-light conditions, but they tend to be more expensive. In contrast, CMOS cameras are smaller, more cost-effective, and consume less power, although they may not perform as well in low light. Both technologies cater to safety and convenience in vehicle parking and reversing scenarios.</p></p>
