<p><strong>The Veterinary Endoscopy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Endoscopy</li><li>Rigid Endoscopy</li><li>Capsule Endoscopy</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy equipment market includes various types tailored for animal healthcare. Flexible endoscopy features malleable scopes allowing access to tight spaces within an animal's body, ideal for procedures like biopsies. Rigid endoscopy, on the other hand, uses stiff instruments for structural visualization and interventions, often employed in surgeries. Capsule endoscopy involves swallowing a small camera capsule that captures images within the gastrointestinal tract, providing a non-invasive diagnostic option. Other equipment may include specialized tools and accessories for diverse veterinary applications.</p></p>
