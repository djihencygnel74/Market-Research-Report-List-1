<p><p>Hepatitis marker testing encompasses various types based on the specific virus: Hepatitis A, B, C, and D. Hepatitis A testing focuses on detecting antibodies indicating past infections or immunization. Hepatitis B testing identifies surface antigens and antibodies to assess infection status and immunity. Hepatitis C marker testing looks for antibodies and viral RNA to diagnose active infection or past exposure. Finally, Hepatitis D testing is conducted mainly in conjunction with Hepatitis B, as it relies on the latter for infection.</p></p>
