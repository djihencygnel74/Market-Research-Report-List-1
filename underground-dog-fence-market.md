<p><p>The underground dog fence market primarily consists of two types: wired and wireless fences. Wired fences involve installing a physical boundary with buried wires that create a defined perimeter, allowing for customizable layouts and reliable containment. In contrast, wireless fences use signals generated by a central transmitter, establishing a circular boundary without physical installation. Both types offer solutions for pet containment, enabling pet owners to keep their dogs safe while allowing freedom to roam within designated areas.</p></p>
