<p><p>The drug addiction treatment market is categorized primarily into three types: opioid addiction, tobacco/nicotine addiction, and alcohol dependence. Opioid addiction treatment focuses on medications like methadone and buprenorphine, addressing withdrawal symptoms and reducing cravings. Tobacco/nicotine addiction treatments utilize nicotine replacement therapy and prescription medications to help individuals quit smoking. Alcohol dependence treatments include counseling, detoxification programs, and medications such as disulfiram or naltrexone to manage withdrawal and reduce relapse rates. Each segment requires tailored approaches to effectively support recovery.</p></p>
