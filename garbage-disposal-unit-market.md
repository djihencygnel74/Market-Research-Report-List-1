<p><p>The garbage disposal unit market primarily features two types: batch feed and continuous feed disposals. Batch feed units require users to load waste into the chamber and activate a stopper to grind the waste in one batch, promoting safety by preventing accidental activation. In contrast, continuous feed units allow users to continuously feed waste while the unit is running, offering convenience but requiring more caution. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, influencing market trends and sales.</p></p>
