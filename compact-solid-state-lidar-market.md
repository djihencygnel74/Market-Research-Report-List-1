<p><strong>The Compact Solid-state LIDAR Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MEMS Based Scanning</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Non-Scanning Flash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compact solid-state LIDAR market encompasses three primary types: MEMS-based scanning, phased array, and non-scanning flash. MEMS-based scanning utilizes micro-electromechanical systems for precise angular scanning, offering high resolution. Phased array LIDAR employs multiple emitting and receiving elements that adjust their phases to steer laser beams, enabling rapid data capture without moving parts. Non-scanning flash LIDAR emits a broad beam of light to capture a scene in one shot, delivering instant depth information, making it ideal for applications requiring quick responsiveness.</p></p>
