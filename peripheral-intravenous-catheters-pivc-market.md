<p><p>The Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) market is segmented into ported and non-ported types. Ported PIVCs feature an access port that allows for multiple intravenous interventions without needing to replace the catheter, enhancing convenience and reducing patient discomfort. Non-ported PIVCs, on the other hand, are simpler in design and are typically used for single infusions. The choice between these types depends on clinical requirements, patient needs, and the duration of intravenous therapy.</p></p>
