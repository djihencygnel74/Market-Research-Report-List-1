<p><p>Low Voltage Switchgear Cabinets (LVSG) are critical components in electrical distribution systems, managing and protecting low voltage circuits. There are two primary types: Fixed Type and Drawer Type. Fixed Type cabinets have components permanently mounted, providing a more compact and robust solution ideal for stable installations. In contrast, Drawer Type cabinets feature movable sections that allow easy access for maintenance and replacement, enhancing flexibility and serviceability. Both types cater to varied applications in commercial and industrial sectors, ensuring efficient power management.</p></p>
