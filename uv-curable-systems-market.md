<p><p>UV curable systems utilize different light sources for curing processes, primarily featuring mercury vapor lamps, fluorescent lamps, and LED technologies. Mercury vapor lamps offer high intensity and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications. Fluorescent lamps provide a cost-effective option with a wider wavelength range but may have lower intensity. LED systems are increasingly popular due to their energy efficiency, longevity, and ability to cure faster with lower heat output, making them ideal for various applications in coatings, inks, and adhesives.</p></p>
