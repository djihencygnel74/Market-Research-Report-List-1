<p><strong>The Spiral Classifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Weir Spiral Classifier</li><li>Submerged Spiral Classifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spiral classifier market is categorized into two main types: high weir spiral classifiers and submerged spiral classifiers. High weir spiral classifiers feature a higher overflow weir, effectively separating finer materials from coarser ones, making them suitable for coarser particle processing. In contrast, submerged spiral classifiers operate with the spiral completely submerged in the slurry, allowing for better separation of fine particles and improving efficiency in operations with fine materials. Both types are essential for mineral processing and sedimentation applications.</p></p>
