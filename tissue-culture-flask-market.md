<p><p>The tissue culture flask market is categorized into three main types: glass, polystyrene, and others. Glass flasks are durable and chemically inert, making them ideal for long-term cell culture but are heavier and more expensive. Polystyrene flasks are lightweight, cost-effective, and designed for single-use applications, enhancing sterility and convenience. Other materials may include polypropylene and specialty plastics, catering to specific research or industry needs. Each type has unique applications in cell biology, microbiology, and pharmaceutical research.</p></p>
