<p><p>The DHA and EPA products market primarily focuses on two main types: triglycerides and ethyl esters. Triglycerides are natural fats found in fish, providing a bioavailable form of omega-3 fatty acids, ideal for dietary supplements. Ethyl esters, on the other hand, are processed forms created through esterification, often offering higher concentrations of DHA and EPA but may require conversion in the body. Both forms are widely used in supplements, functional foods, and nutraceuticals, catering to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
