<p><p>The microneedles market for transdermal and intradermal drug delivery comprises three primary types: solid, hollow, and dissolvable microneedles. Solid microneedles deliver drugs in solid format by penetrating the skin, suitable for vaccines and large molecules. Hollow microneedles allow fluid passage, facilitating the release of liquid formulations directly into the skin. Dissolvable microneedles dissolve upon insertion, releasing the drug as they dissolve, offering a pain-free and efficient delivery method, particularly for sensitive medications and vaccines.</p></p>
