<p><strong>The Asphalt Recycling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asphalt Hot Recycling System</li><li>Asphalt Cold Recycling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asphalt recycling system market is primarily divided into two types: Asphalt Hot Recycling Systems and Asphalt Cold Recycling Systems. </p><p>Asphalt Hot Recycling Systems involve heating the reclaimed asphalt pavement (RAP) to high temperatures, allowing for a more flexible and homogeneous mixture, which is then reused in new asphalt production. </p><p>In contrast, Asphalt Cold Recycling Systems utilize emulsification or foaming techniques at ambient temperatures, making it a more environmentally friendly option, suitable for in-situ repairs and low-temperature applications. Both systems enhance sustainability and reduce costs in road construction.</p></p>
